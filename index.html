SILVER BROWNS

Artist

Silver Browns is an uprising South African Artist dominating in the Lekompo genre, founded in Limpopo Province.

His music is inspired by the likes of Makhadzi, King Monada, Chris Brown, and others. Following up on their footsteps but bringing a new-age Lekompo sound. His career started in the early months in 2019, where he was guided and mentored while participating in the Limpopo Merit Awards after-school programme led by Joyce 'Majoy' Ledwaba from Skeem Saam.

Reviving on that, he decided to take his music career further, after earning two certificates in Acting & Dancing along with a trophy and medal. For safety reasons, he couldn't yet disclose his age and other personal details, according to (anonymous).

The fans believes there's so much talent to discover in this young man, as the Lekompo genre is expanding across several provinces and could soon become a national sound.
